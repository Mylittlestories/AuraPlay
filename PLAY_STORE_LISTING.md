## Full Description (4000 chars max)
🎵 AuraPlay — Professional Offline Music Player

AuraPlay is a premium music player designed for audiophiles and music lovers who demand the best sound quality from their Android device.

🎧 PROFESSIONAL AUDIO
• 10-band parametric equalizer with 10 presets
• 8 Tube Amplifier simulations (McIntosh, Fender, Benchmark, Denafrips, Marantz)
• Bass Boost, Virtualizer, Loudness Enhancer
• Playback speed control (0.5x – 2.0x)
• Replay Gain volume normalization

🎨 WINAMP-INSPIRED VISUALS
• Real-time spectrum analyzer
• 8 beautiful themes (Winamp Classic, Cyberpunk, Vinyl Retro, and more)
• Rotating vinyl disc animation
• Blurred album art backgrounds

🔀 SMART SHUFFLE
• 8 shuffle algorithms (Smart, Artist Mix, Album Mix, Genre Mix, Discovery)
• No repetition, varied listening experience
• Weighted by play count for personalized playback

📋 FULL LIBRARY MANAGEMENT
• Browse by Tracks, Albums, Artists, Genres, Folders
• Create and manage playlists
• Favorites system
• Instant search
• Detailed metadata viewer (bitrate, sample rate, format)

🔒 100% OFFLINE & PRIVATE
• No internet connection required
• No data collection
• No ads, no tracking
• Your music stays on your device

✨ ADDITIONAL FEATURES
• Sleep timer (15 min to 2 hours)
• Background playback with notification controls
• Lock screen controls
• Bluetooth device support
• Android 13+ compatible
